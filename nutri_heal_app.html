Honey-Ginger-Lemon Decoction
5 ingredients · 5 minutes · Serves 1
Ingredients
🍯 Honey — 2 tsp
🫚 Fresh ginger — 1 inch
🍋 Lemon — ½ piece
🌶 Black pepper — 3 corns
💧 Water — 1 cup
Voice Guide Ready
Tap to start step-by-step cooking guide
1
Boil water — Pour 1 cup of water into a saucepan and bring it to a rolling boil on medium-high heat.
2
Add ginger — Peel and crush a 1-inch piece of fresh ginger. Drop it into the boiling water.
3
Add pepper — Lightly crush 3 black peppercorns and add to the pot. Simmer on low for 3 minutes.
4
Cool & add honey — Remove from heat. Let cool for 2 minutes so honey nutrients are preserved. Stir in 2 tsp honey.
5
Squeeze lemon — Add juice of ½ a lemon. Stir well and pour into a cup. Drink warm.

Steps
Step 0 of 5
1
Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan on medium-high heat until you see rolling bubbles.
2
Peel and crush 1 inch of fresh ginger using the flat of your knife. Add it to the boiling water.
3
Crush 3 black peppercorns and add to the pot. Reduce flame to low. Let it simmer for 3 minutes.
4
Turn off the heat. Wait 2 minutes. Then stir in 2 tsp of raw honey — never add honey to boiling liquid.
5
Squeeze in juice from half a lemon. Stir gently. Pour into a cup and sip slowly while warm. You're done!
